# Code of Conduct
This code of conduct applies in all spaces managed by [Elypia](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/12203025), including our
repositories on [GitLab](https://gitlab.com/Elypia) and [GitHub](https://github.com/elypia), 
any social media channels we administrate such as our [Discord](https://discordapp.com/invite/hprGMaM) guild or [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/+elypia:matrix.org) channels, and any other communication used by our community.

## Guidelines
### Transparency
We try our best to be transparent with the community and only do things within the
community interest. It's not possible to make everyone happy, but at the very least
there is no room for selfishness. Public conversations should take place and
nothing adverse to the community should be performed without justification.

### Inclusivity
We shouldn't gatekeep people out of projects or the community, everyone's invited
to have fun and participate. No one should be discriminated against because of [protected
characteristics](https://www.gov.uk/discrimination-your-rights), their level of knowledge, 
political opinion, or even petty things like if someone likes using a mouse.

### Mature Content
Any public communication should be considered safe for work (SFW). This means
that bug reports, feature requests, or any other form of public communication should
not contain inappropriate media regardless of format without prompt notice or warning
and consent from whoever will consume it.

If it is necessary for whatever reason to share mature content, it must be done in
a designated place, privately, or as a [confidential issue](https://docs.gitlab.com/ce/user/project/issues/confidential_issues.html) 
if on GitLab.

## Reporting Problems
If you have any problems with a member of the community,
please contact us through one of the channels below or contact the managing director 
directly if it's something you want handled privately, even from the team.

* Ask us on [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/+elypia:matrix.org) or [Discord](https://discordapp.com/invite/hprGMaM)!
* Email support@elypia.org.
* Email [Seth](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/officers/JHIszHq0eErcApZ9k_FhzBFPF8A/appointments) (seth@elypia.org) the current managing director.
