# Contributing
There are many ways to contribute to our projects depending on what skills you have.

## How To Contribute
* You can help develop free and open-source software by fixing bugs, or adding features
to existing libraries or applications.
* You can create assets like emotes, or marketing material under a
[Creative Commons](https://creativecommons.org/) license.
* You can help us translate projects via our [Weblate](https://i18n.elypia.org/) instance.
* You can support us financially by [sponsoring](https://github.com/sponsors/elypia)
us on [GitHub](https://github.com/) or donating through one of the means listed on our website.
* Share our projects or posts on social media and help people find us!
