# Support
If you just have problems with one of our projects and need support,
consider using a forum or contacting us rather than creating an issue.

* Ask us on [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/+elypia:matrix.org) or [Discord](https://discordapp.com/invite/hprGMaM)!
* Send an email to support@elypia.org.
* Post the question on [StackOverflow](https://stackoverflow.com/).

## Security
If your problem is security related, please go to the [security](SECURITY.md) page.
